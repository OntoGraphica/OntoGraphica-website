Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical engagement take?

Discovery and design take 2-3 weeks. Implementation and validation typically take 2-3 months depending on complexity. Knowledge transfer is ongoing.

When will OntoCanvas be available?

OntoCanvas is in active development and will launch mid-2026. Join our early access program to get exclusive launch pricing and direct product input.

Do you work with existing enterprise systems?

Yes. We specialize in integrating ontologies with existing systems like CRM, ERP, data warehouses, and knowledge graphs. No rip-and-replace required.

What if we're new to ontologies?

That's our specialty. We've worked with organizations brand new to semantic infrastructure. We start with education, then design, then implementation.

Can we build a pilot first?

Absolutely. Many clients start with a focused pilot on a single domain or use case to prove ROI before broader implementation.

What's your pricing model?

We offer custom proposals based on scope, complexity, and team size. Contact us for a consultation to discuss your specific needs and budget.
